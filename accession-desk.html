This desk composes one JSON row on your screen and stores nothing — refresh and it forgets. Fill it from the student’s own paper layer, copy the row into data/bestiary.json (newest last), add a plate to plates/ (a screenshot, tools/capture.mjs, or the student’s own field sketch), commit, push. The creature appears.

Accession requires the collector’s consent. First names or field-names only. De-accession is immediate on request.

The Nomenclator

Deals Latin-ish binomials — dog Latin, proudly: these are names, not claims. The collector always has final say. Genus goes by what the creature does; species by where it lives or how it behaves.
